Notes App

Application allows you to quickly note your thoughts or any other textual information you can enter with keyboard. Just plain text, nothing else. Its list of permissions contains only Network Communication.

Download

You can download APK file for 0.0.5 version. Works on Android 4.1.1 or newer.
